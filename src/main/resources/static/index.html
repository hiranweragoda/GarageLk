About GarageLK

Connecting Vehicle Owners with Elite Automotive Services Across Sri Lanka

GarageLK is Sri Lanka's premier digital marketplace designed specifically to bridge the gap between automotive service providers and vehicle owners. Whether you need routine hybrid diagnostics, specialized wheel alignment, or urgent roadside assistance, we bring verified professionals directly to your fingertips.

Our mission is to bring transparency, efficiency, and reliability to the Sri Lankan automotive service ecosystem. We empower garage owners to scale their businesses while offering customers a seamless search-and-booking platform coupled with a verified community review system.
